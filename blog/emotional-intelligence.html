1. What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Interlligence is one's ability to sense, understand and react to the surrounding world and the feelings of themselves as well as the others and interact with them.

"Emotional intelligence refers to your ability to recognize and regulate emotion, and to use social awareness in problem-solving." - Rebecca Joy Stanborough 2021

"The ability to monitor one’s own and other’s feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guide one’s thinking and actions.” - Salovey and Mayer 1990

2. How is EQ different to IQ?

EQ is about emotions and how well they can understand and work with others whereas IQ is about knowledge, skills and the ability to solve problems.

3. Why is EQ important?

Because EQ is the ability to communicate and work with others which are usually required in large scale projects.
